Fun facts about me

😊 Hey there! I'm Riya Hajare, a UX designer with a passion for creating digital experiences that not only look stunning but also feel intuitive and delightful. I believe the best designs are born from understanding people, and that's exactly what drives me — making tech feel natural and enjoyable for everyone.

💡 I’ve been drawing and painting since I was five, and my natural talent in art led me to pursue a career in design. I began my formal graphic design education at a community college while discovering my passion for UX Design through BRIDGEGOOD UX Design Apprenticeship in 2019. Since then, I have shifted my career focus to UX design.

💻 Every project I take on is an opportunity to solve problems in creative ways, and I’m always excited to learn something new. If you’re looking for a UX designer who’s passionate, curious, and loves bringing fresh ideas to life — let’s connect! I’d love to collaborate and create something amazing together.

🍵 When I'm not designing, you'll probably find me traveling to new places, exploring local gems, or spending quality time with family and friends. Whether it’s getting lost in a new city, discovering a hidden café, or simply having fun outdoors, I love how these experiences spark creativity and new ideas. Life's about enjoying the journey, and that mindset flows into the work I do.
